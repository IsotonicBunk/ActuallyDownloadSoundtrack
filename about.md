# ActuallyDownloadSoundtrack
Download Newgrounds song by direct link!

# What does this mod?
Have you ever tried to click "Download soundtrack" in song info menu?

![awesome picture of "download soundtrack" button](isotonicbunk.actually-download-soundtrack/awesome-picture.png)

This button redirects you to the [Newgrounds](https://www.newgrounds.com/) song page. [(Example)](https://www.newgrounds.com/audio/listen/723714)

But this mod makes this button redirect directly to the song file. [(Example)](https://audio.ngfiles.com/723000/723714_Epilogue.mp3)

This also works with songs that hosted on Robtop's servers. (like [music by Acid-Notation](https://geometrydashcontent.b-cdn.net/songs/708694.mp3))
